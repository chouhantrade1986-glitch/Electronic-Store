Accessibility Statement

1. Our Commitment

ElectroMart is committed to providing a website experience that is inclusive and usable for all visitors.

2. Standards and Practices

We continuously improve our pages with accessibility best practices for structure, readability, and keyboard support.

3. Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is part of our ongoing development process and is reviewed as new features are added.

4. Assistive Technology Compatibility

We aim to maintain compatibility with modern browsers and commonly used assistive technologies.

5. Known Limitations

Some content may still be improving; we prioritize fixes based on impact and user feedback.

6. Contact Us

If you face accessibility barriers, please contact support through your account or orders page so we can assist.
