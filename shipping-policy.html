Shipping Policy

Effective date: March 2, 2026

1. Processing Time

Most orders are processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation.

2. Delivery Timeframes

Standard delivery usually takes 3-7 business days, while expedited options may arrive faster by region.

3. Shipping Charges

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on destination, package weight, and selected delivery speed.

4. Tracking Orders

Once shipped, tracking details are shared through your orders page and registered contact channels.

5. Delays and Exceptions

Delays can occur due to weather, regional disruptions, address issues, or carrier constraints.

6. Failed Delivery Attempts

If delivery fails, carriers may retry or hold the package for pickup depending on local carrier policy.

7. International or Restricted Deliveries

Availability of cross-border shipping varies by product category and destination regulations.

8. Contact for Shipping Support

For shipment-related questions, contact support through your account and orders pages.
