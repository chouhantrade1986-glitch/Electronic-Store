Frequently Asked Questions

Updated: March 2, 2026

1. How can I track my order?

Go to the Orders page and search by order ID or product name to view status updates.

2. What payment methods are accepted?

Accepted methods depend on your profile settings and checkout availability for your region.

3. Can I cancel an order?

Orders can usually be canceled before shipment. Once shipped, use the return and refund policy flow.

4. How long does shipping take?

Delivery times vary by location and method. See the Shipping Policy page for full details.

5. How do refunds work?

Refund eligibility and timelines are explained on the Refund Policy page.

6. How do I contact support?

Use your Account or Orders pages to access support options and request assistance.
