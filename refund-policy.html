Refund Policy

Effective date: March 2, 2026

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available for eligible returned items that meet product condition and return window requirements.

2. Refund Request Timeline

Refund requests should be initiated within the stated return period from the delivery date.

3. Refund Method

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method unless otherwise required by policy or regulation.

4. Processing Duration

After approval, refund settlement may take 5-10 business days depending on payment provider timelines.

5. Non-Refundable Cases

Items with physical damage from misuse, missing major accessories, or policy exclusions may be denied.

6. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may apply for opened or incomplete items where full-value return criteria are not met.

7. Cancelled Orders

Cancelled prepaid orders are refunded automatically after cancellation confirmation.

8. Support

For refund status and disputes, use the support options available in your account and orders pages.
